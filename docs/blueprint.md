# **App Name**: Girls Store.

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog Display: Present products with images, names, prices, and categories in a clean, scrollable interface, matching the visual style of the provided image.
- Product Data Persistence: Securely store and manage all product details, including descriptions, categories, and inventory information.
- Category Filtering: Allow users to filter the product catalog using interactive category tabs (e.g., 'All', 'Face', 'Nails') as depicted.
- Shopping Cart Management: Enable users to add, update quantities, and remove products from a dynamic shopping cart, with a clear summary of selected items and total price.
- WhatsApp Direct Order: Provide a mechanism for users to submit their complete shopping cart details via a pre-filled WhatsApp message to the store owner.
- AI Product Inquiry Tool: An AI tool that provides instant, relevant answers to user questions about specific product characteristics or uses, leveraging available product data.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant magenta pink (#EB168C) for headlines, prominent text, and interactive elements, conveying a bold and feminine aesthetic.
- Background color: A soft, heavily desaturated light pink (#FFF0F6), chosen to maintain a gentle and clean feel while being visibly related to the primary hue.
- Accent color: A deep violet (#681B89) analogous to the primary hue, used subtly to provide sophisticated contrast for minor details or hover states.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for its contemporary, geometric precision and readability, aligning with the fashion-forward style.
- Minimalist and rounded icons, consistent with the clean and modern aesthetic observed in the provided design, like the floating cart icon.
- Card-based product display with rounded corners and ample spacing for an elegant, uncluttered presentation, centered branding, and horizontal category filters.
- Subtle and smooth transitions for category filtering and product card interactions, enhancing user experience without being distracting.
